Almost 9pm Zulu; let's talk about colors.

Red - Doq
Green - SuD
Light blue - SuD 'ej wov
Yellow - SuD
Orange - Doq
Brown - Doq 'ej wovbe'
Purple - Doq 'ej SuD
Black - qIj
Gray - qIj 'ej wov
White - chIS
Pink - Doqqu' 'ej wov
Dark blue - SuD 'ej Hurgh

-=-=-
Previously, I had been using {Doq 'ej 'Iw rur} for "pink".
