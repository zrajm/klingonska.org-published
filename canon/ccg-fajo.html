Decipher Inc. Trading Card from The Fajo Collection

[Collector’s list of all cards in this series]

One of the cards released in the The Fajo Collection of the Star Trek Customizable Card Game was written entirely in Klingon (an English translation was, however, provided in the rulebook accompanying the deck).

The text on the card consists of a description of how to use the card in game play, copyright text, and some proverbs taken from TKW. The original Klingon is of high quality and this have led people to speculate that this card was translated by Marc Okrand, but when asked about it (by Zrajm, in 2013) he could neither confirm nor deny his involvement.
