SkyBox Trading Cards for TNG Season 4

[Collector’s list of all cards in this series]

Three of the cards in the SkyBox trading card set for Star Trek: The Next Generation fourth season had Klingon text specially translated by Marc Okrand. They are here faithfully reproduced with all errors occuring in the originals. For more details on which these errors are, see the plain text transcriptions of card S19, card S20, and card S21. A transcript of these cards was also printed in Hol­QeD 5:2, page 14 in June 1996.

SkyBox Trading Card S19

Front, card S19 Back, card S19

S19: Qum­wI’ – Klingon Communicator

lab­meH ’ev­nagh Se’ lo’ tlhI­ngan Qum­wI’. jan­mey ngo’ lu­lo’­lu’­DI’ pIj jab­bI­’ID nIS­pu’ woj.

A KlingonTM communicator sends a signal through subspace radio. Older models were susceptible to radiation.

S20: Ha’­quj – KlingonTM Sash

qor­Du’­Daj tuq ’oS Ha’­qu­je­’e’ tuQ­bogh wo’­rIv. tuQ­taH­vIS Hem. ghaH­vaD quH­Daj qaw­moH.

The sash that WorfTM wears is a symbol of his family’s house. He wears it proudly as a reminder of his heritage.

S21: Soj – KlingonTM Food

yIn­taH­bogh tlhI­ngan Soj tlhol jab­lu’­DI’ tIv­qu’­lu’. qagh, ro’­qegh­’I­wa­chab, targh tIq Sop ’e’ lu­ngIl Hu­man­pu’ puS.

Klingon food is best when served fresh and live. Few humans dare to eat gagh, rokeg blood pie, or heart of targ.
