Message to Kronos
=================

This is a video recording of Marc Okrand reading an invitation for Klingons to
come watch the performance of {'u'} -- “The First Authentic Klingon Opera on
Earth”, first performed in The Hague, September 10, 2010. This source commonly
referred to as *{'u'}-MTK* or just *MTK*.

28, 2010):

  <tr>

    <td>{'u' 'oH ghe'naQ pong.} [sic!]
      <br><<The name of the opera is {'u'}.>>
  <tr>
    <td>{yuQmajDaq may' ghe'naQ wa'DIch wIQoybogh 'oH 'u'.} [sic!]

      <br><<When, you ask?>>
  <tr>
    <td>{poH tuj bI'reS nungbogh wa' jaj qeylIS DIS chorghvatlh loSmaH jav qaStaHvIS.} [sic!]
      <br><<In the days that follow the summer solstice in the Year of Kahless 846.>>
  <tr>
    <td>{ghe'naQ Daqvo' QInvam wIlab.}
      <br><<We will place a homing device transmitting this message at the site of the opera.>>
