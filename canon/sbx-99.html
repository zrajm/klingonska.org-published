SkyBox Trading Card for DS9 Season 1

[Collector’s list of all cards in this series]

One of the cards in the SkyBox trading card set for the first season of Star Trek: Deep Space Nine was produced in six different versions, with different languages: English (card 01), German (card 95), French (card 96), Spanish (card 97), Japanese (card 98), and Klingon (card 99).

Marc Okrand translated the Klingon card. It is here exactly reproduced, with all errors occuring in the original. For more details on which these errors are, see plain text transcription of card 99.

A transcript of card 99 was also printed in Hol­QeD 3:2, (p. 9) in June 1994.
