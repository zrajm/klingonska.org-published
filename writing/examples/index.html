Some Pictures of pI­qaD

These are some pictures of the different styles of pI­qaD that I have encountered, mainly those used by Nick Nicholas, Roger Cheesbro and myself. It is my intention to write a more comprehensive description of the Klingon writing system with detailed information about different styles of longhand as used by klingonists and Klingons around the galaxy. (Sooner or later.) The main research for this article consists of these pictures, but as of now the pictures will have to speak for themselves.

If you have anything that you wish to contribute, you are more than welcome to contact zrajm, and share what you’ve got. All contributions will, of course, be fully credited.
